Scattered records

  • 193 national parliaments worldwide
  • 664,000 subnational governments (regions, provinces, municipalities)
  • Each publishing its own websites and registers in its own language and format

Sources: IPU Parline · World Observatory on Subnational Government

Constant flux

  • 80 national elections in 2026 alone
  • Regional and local elections nobody bothers to count
  • Between elections: appointments, reshuffles, resignations, deaths

Source: IFES ElectionGuide

Our bet: Wikidata

  • Community-maintained
  • Outlives any single team
  • Free, multilingual, unique identifiers

The goal

Make Wikidata the definitive source for who holds power — free for everyone.

How we get there

  1. Capture the web (evidence archived)
  2. Machines extract candidate facts
  3. Humans verify
  4. Confirmed data flows to Wikidata

PoliLoom

  • Shows a claim + the exact source passage it came from
  • You confirm or reject
  • Confirmed edits go to Wikidata under your account
  • No Wikidata expertise needed
