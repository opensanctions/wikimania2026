Where do you draw the line?

Everyone who holds political office — elected or appointed, at any level.

Our answer, plainly: holding political office. Edge cases like candidates and judges are still open — we're figuring it out as we go. PEP ≠ politician: compliance lists watch anyone powerful. Segue: the line decides who gets watched, checked, and held accountable — that's why this data matters.

Why political data matters
